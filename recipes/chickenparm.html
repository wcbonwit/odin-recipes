Chicken Parmesan

Beautiful Chicken Parmesan

This recipe is great for chefs of all levels. It is a simple recipe that really sticks to the basics of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup panco breadcrumbs
  • 4 half-pound chicken filets
  • 1 jar tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Lay out flour, eggs, and panco breadcrumbs in separate bowls/pans
  3. Take chicken filets and cover in flour
  4. Then dip chicken into egg mixture
  5. Then cover chicken in breadcrumbs
